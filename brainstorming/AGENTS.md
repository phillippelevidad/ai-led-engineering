# Brainstorming

Temporary drafts and raw thinking. Not canonical.

## Purpose

Quick capture: language standards, open questions, partial rubrics, notes.

## Lifecycle

1. Write freely.
2. Promote to `research/` when reviewed and intended to persist.
3. Delete or leave a pointer after promotion.

Do not build skills from here.

## Conventions

One topic per file; kebab-case names. Mark uncertainty (`TBD`). Link related research when it exists.

## Promoting to research

Move to `research/<topic>/`. Use RFC 2119 (MUST / SHOULD / MAY). Add **Enforced by** for load-bearing rules.

## Do not

Cite as standards, duplicate `research/`, or let stale drafts accumulate.
